Our Story

Palm Oil, Done Right

We didn't reinvent palm oil. We decided it deserved better.

There is nothing complicated about what we do. We source palm oil — the kind that has fed families, fueled kitchens, and built livelihoods across Nigeria for generations. What we have chosen to complicate, deliberately, is the standard of how it reaches you.

Synthova Oil was built on a straightforward conviction: that the most essential products deserve the most rigorous care. Palm oil is not a luxury. It is a staple — present at the base of nearly every meal that matters in this part of the world. And yet, for too long, the experience of sourcing it has been defined by inconsistency. Variable quality. Uncertain supply. No guarantee that what you ordered today will match what you received last time.

We exist to close that gap. Every batch of Synthova Oil is handled with an attention to process that most people reserve for premium imports. Deliberate sourcing, deliberate storage, and deliberate delivery — because when something shows up in your kitchen, your business, or your production line, it should show up right.

Whether you are a household buying for the week, a food vendor running a tight operation, or a business procuring at scale, Synthova Oil is built to serve you without friction. Consistent product. Honest pricing. A supply you can plan around.

FAQ

Common Questions

How is delivery handled and what are the costs?
We deliver nationwide across Nigeria. Delivery fees are calculated based on your location and order size. For orders within Benin City (Edo State), delivery is ₦500. For other states, fees range from ₦1,200–₦3,500. Use our delivery calculator on the checkout page for an instant estimate.
How should I store Synthova Palm Oil?
Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, keep the bottle tightly sealed. Properly stored, our palm oil maintains peak quality for up to 12 months. For best flavour, use within 6 months of opening.
